  <ul>

<li>The Drivers & Strategies Behind Voice Peering
<li>IPv6: Impact on Peering & Interconnection
<li>Threat analysis on key interconnection points and systems
<li>Transitioning Carrier Wholesale Least-Cost-Routing Systems to ENUM

<li>Re-architecting the Internet for Sustainability: Photonic & Ethernet Core
